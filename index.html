Welcome the Serial Engineering, a place for developing ideas one at a time.

This website is a work in progress. Who knows what the future holds?

This website is dedicated to serial communication, the science of transmitting information from one place to another, one bit at a time. Serial communications were employed by humankind long before the advent of information theory. With advances in technology it is now ubiquitous to modern life. In the space of one lifetime the speed and reliability of data transfers have become borderline magical. At the same time, legacy serial interfaces have not changed much and continue to be employed in modern systems precisely because, well, they simply work. One could imagine how the pioneers of the field would react if they could observe the most mundane of miracles such as backing up a smartphone or streaming live sports.
